Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our Verification Service ("the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you link your Roblox account to a Discord identity using our OAuth2 flow.

Transparency Note: We only request the information necessary to verify that you own the Roblox account you claim to own. We do not have access to your Roblox password or sensitive billing information.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize our application via Roblox, we receive specific data from the Roblox API, including:

  • Roblox UserID: The unique number associated with your account.
  • Username & Display Name: Your current identifying names on the platform.
  • Account Metadata: Publicly available info such as account age, badge count, or group memberships (used for server requirements).
  • Discord Identity: Your Discord User ID, which is linked to your Roblox data in our database.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity within a Discord community.
  • To automatically assign Discord roles based on your Roblox group rank or assets.
  • To prevent "ban evasion" and maintain server security.

3. Data Retention

Your data is stored securely for as long as you are a member of the Discord server utilizing our Service. If you leave the server or revoke the application's access via your Roblox security settings, your linked data may be queued for deletion.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Your linked profile is visible only to the Administrators of the Discord server you are verifying in. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to request a copy of the data we have stored about you or to request its immediate deletion from our database.

How to contact us: To exercise your data rights or report a privacy concern, please make a ticket in our Discord server. Our support team will process your request within 48 hours.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) to protect your data during transmission. While we take every precaution, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we encourage users to maintain strong security settings on both Roblox and Discord.
