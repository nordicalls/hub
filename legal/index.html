How we use your data

Nordicalls is a verification tool. We only collect what is necessary to link your Discord account to your Roblox account.

1. Data Collected

When you authorize via Roblox OAuth2, we receive:

  • Your Roblox UserID (to identify who you are).
  • Your Username and Display Name.

2. Purpose

This data is used exclusively to assign roles in linked Discord servers. We do not track your activity on Roblox, and we never see your password.

3. Data Deletion

You are in control. To wipe your data from Nordicalls:

  • Revoke access in your Roblox Settings > Connected Apps.
  • Or, email nordically.dev@gmail.com with your UserID for manual removal.
