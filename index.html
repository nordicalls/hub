community

nordicalls focuses on building a safe, friendly, and welcoming community. if you every feel unsafe in our server, one of our comment sections, or any partnered servers, please create a ticket immediately. we prioritise member safety over everything else.

our powerful moderation tools help prevent any attacks or hate speech before it spreads. the quicker you inform or grab the attention of a moderator, the quicker it can be resolved with ease.

polls

we incorporate our server members in design and update polls, so that everyone can get a say in what happens at nordicalls.

polls are always at a base level. if we believe a decision should be made by community oversight, we will keep that poll and discussion about that poll private until revealed to the public.

terms

failure to follow our terms can lead to a removal from the server or worse. our terms can update regularly and we are not required to inform members of our terms. external action taken against nordicalls can result in punishment via the nordicalls server if we deem it necessary.
